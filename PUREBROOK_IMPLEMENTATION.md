# Purebrook Kitchens - Premium Custom Cabinetry Website

## Implementation Summary

A professional, premium website for Purebrook Kitchens showcasing custom cabinetry services with elegant design, comprehensive process transparency, and seamless consultation booking.

## Pages Implemented

### 1. Home Page (`/`)
- **Hero Section**: Full-screen hero with stunning kitchen transformation image
- **Value Proposition**: Three-column feature showcase (Custom Design, Premium Materials, Complete Process)
- **Featured Projects**: Grid of 3 highlighted projects with hover effects
- **Process Overview**: 4-phase timeline with visual indicators
- **Call-to-Action**: Prominent consultation booking section

### 2. Process Page (`/process`)
- **Process Hero**: Craftsman at work imagery
- **Timeline Overview**: 10-week complete process breakdown
- **Detailed Phases**: 4 phases with step-by-step details, images, and deliverables
  - Phase 1: Consultation & Planning (2 weeks)
  - Phase 2: Design Development (3 weeks)
  - Phase 3: In-House Manufacturing (4 weeks)
  - Phase 4: Professional Installation (1 week)
- **Quality Commitments**: Premium materials, master craftsmanship showcase
- **Before & After Gallery**: Visual transformation demonstration

### 3. Gallery & Contact Page (`/gallery`)
- **Project Gallery**: Detailed portfolio with images, descriptions, and client testimonials
- **Category Filtering**: All Projects, Kitchen, Living Room, Storage Solutions
- **Before & After Section**: Side-by-side transformation comparison
- **Contact Section**:
  - Contact information (phone, email, service area)
  - Comprehensive consultation booking form with validation
  - Project type, timeline, and budget selection
  - Success/error state handling

## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary (Gold)**: `hsl(42 88% 55%)` - Premium cabinet hardware gold accent
- **Background**: `hsl(30 40% 98%)` - Warm white/cream
- **Foreground**: `hsl(25 15% 20%)` - Deep charcoal text
- **Secondary**: `hsl(30 25% 85%)` - Warm wood tone
- **Accent**: `hsl(25 15% 25%)` - Deep charcoal for emphasis

### Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - Elegant, sophisticated
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) - Clean, readable
- **Scale**: 3.5rem (h1) down to 1rem (h6) with proper hierarchy

### Design Elements
- Warm, premium aesthetic matching high-end cabinetry
- Sophisticated serif headings paired with clean sans-serif body text
- Gold accent colors reflecting cabinet hardware
- Professional photography with hover effects
- Smooth transitions and polished interactions

## Components

### Core Components
- **Header**: Fixed navigation with logo, menu, phone number, and CTA button
- **Footer**: Company info, quick links, contact details, service area
- **ContactForm**: Multi-field form with validation and state management
  - Name, email, phone (required)
  - Project type, timeline, budget (dropdowns)
  - Message textarea
  - Success/error feedback
